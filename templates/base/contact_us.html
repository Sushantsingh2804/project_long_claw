About Us


Email us: plasmapal@gmail.com


With PlasmaPal, you can donate your plasma to those in need, or find someone to who can donate you lifesaving plasma! PlasmaPal was founded after getting a text from our friend about how she was looking for COVID-19 recovered patients who could donate plasma as it could help save her mother's life. This was very heartbreaking, so we made this platform to connect those individuals.

About Us



Email us: plasmapal@gmail.com


We decided to make PlasmaPal to help individuals save lives. After getting a text from our friend about how she was looking for a COVID-19 recovered patient's plasma as it could help save her mother's life. This was very heartbreaking, so we came up with this idea to connect those individuals on a platform meant to save lives. With PlasmaPal, you can donate your plasma to those in need, or find someone to who can donate you lifesaving plasma!

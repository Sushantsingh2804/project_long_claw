How your Plasma could save someone's life


According to the FDA, If you have fully recovered from COVID-19, you may be able to help patients currently fighting the infection by donating your plasma. Because you fought the infection, your plasma now contains COVID-19 antibodies. These antibodies provided one way for your immune system to fight the virus when you were sick, so your plasma may be able to be used to help others fight off the disease.

For Donors


If you are a COVID-19 recovered patient, your plasma could help treat someone affected by the virus!


1. Sign up and fill out all your information accurately.


2. We will verify your COVID-19 test results


3. Patients who are a match will request to match with you.


4. We will reach out to you with the patient information so you can reach out to them and help them battle COVID-19!

For Patients

If you are a patient battling COVID-19, a recovered patient's plasma could help you defeat the virus!


1. Sign up and fill out all your information accurately.


2. Use the search function to look up eligible donors


3. You can request to be matched with eligible donors.


4. We will reach out to the donor with your information so they can reach out to you and help you fight COVID-19!
